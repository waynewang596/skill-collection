# Guotai Haitong Document Structure Contract

## Document Type
Securities research reports (证券研究报告) from Guotai Haitong Securities / Haitong International.

---

## Section Hierarchy

### Level 1: Main Sections
Numbered with Arabic numerals: `1.`, `2.`, `3.`, etc.
Format: **Bold, brand blue `#00509c`, 14-16pt**
Example: `1. AI行业动态`, `2. AI应用资讯`, `3. AI大模型资讯`

### Level 2: Sub-headings
Individual news items, analysis points, or topics.
Format: **Bold, black `#000000`, 12-13pt**
Example: `上海发布具身智能产业发展实施方案`, `Qwen团队开源两款4B端侧模型`

### Level 3: Body Text
Paragraph content describing the news or analysis.
Format: Regular weight, near-black `#333333`, 10.5-11pt, justified alignment

## Common Structural Patterns

### Section Heading Pattern
```
1. Section Title (blue #00509c, bold, 14-16pt)

   Sub-heading (bold, black #000000, 12-13pt)
   Body text paragraph with detailed information...
   Source attribution, key facts, metrics, quotes.
   May continue for multiple lines.

   Another Sub-heading (bold, black)
   Another body text paragraph...
```

### News Item Pattern
```
News Title (bold, black, ~13pt)
据[source][platform] [date]消息，[company/organization] [action]...
Details continue in regular body text, justified alignment.
May include specific numbers, metrics, and quotes.
Paragraph spans full content width.
```

### Risk Warning Pattern
```
X. 风险提示 (blue #00509c, bold section heading)

    Risk factor summary in bold, followed by detailed explanation
    in regular body text spanning one or more paragraphs.
```

## Cross-Reference Rules

- Sections referenced by number: "详见第3节", "参见第1.2节"
- Reports referenced by title + date: "【新材料产业周报】... 2025.08.11"
- Analyst certifications referenced by registration number
- Rating definitions cross-referenced in disclosure section

---

## Page Break Rules

1. Cover page is always page 1 (footer shows "1 of Y")
2. Table of Contents is page 2
3. Each main section starts on a new page when space is insufficient
4. Risk Warning section starts on a new page
5. Legal disclaimer starts on a new page (may span multiple pages)
6. No orphaned headings (heading must stay with at least 2 lines of following content)
7. Sub-headings should not be separated from their first body paragraph

## Content Patterns

### Industry Tracking Report (产业跟踪)
- Multiple news items grouped by category
- Each item: source attribution, date, key facts, impact analysis
- Categories: AI industry dynamics, AI applications, large model updates, frontier tech
- Dense information flow, continuous text

### Equity Research Report (股票研究)
- Investment thesis with recommendation
- Executive summary with key investment points
- Detailed analysis sections
- Risk factors
- Extensive disclosure and compliance sections (rating tables, regional notices)
